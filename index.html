Thank you so much for stopping by!

I invite you to browse through this site to see examples of custom, hand-painted watercolor portraits of homes, along with digital backgrounds for newborn photography and children's book illustrations — I would love to discuss your personalized watercolor home portrait or surface pattern design or illustration project you might have in mind. 

With a BFA in Illustration, my professional experience includes character design, prop design, and storyboards, working for more than 10 years in the animation industry in Los Angeles, where I collaborated with creative teams at the Warner Bros. and Nickelodeon studios. My credits include the feature film Space Jam and Nickelodeon TV shows such as "Dora the Explorer," "Hey Arnold," and "Oswald the Octopus."

I'm looking forward to meeting you and discussing your ideas and projects, I'm open to licensing and illustration for Surface pattern design.

A native of Sydney, Australia, I currently reside in sunny Pasadena California, with my husband and two crazy-sweet boys.

 Let's connect- Contact me.


-Elyse
