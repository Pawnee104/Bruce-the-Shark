Thank you for stopping by!

Browse through this site to see examples of custom, hand-painted watercolor portraits of homes and pets, children's book illustrations, and fashion illustration -- I would love to discuss your custom portrait or any illustration project you might have.

I have a BFA in Illustration and over 10 years' experience in the Animation Industry in Los Angeles -- at the Warner Bros. and Nickelodeon studios -- working in collaboration with many creative teams. My project experience includes character design, prop design, and storyboards.

I have worked on the Warner Bros. feature film Space Jam, as well as TV shows such as "Dora the Explorer," "Hey Arnold" and "Oswald the Octopus."

As a children's book illustrator, I was thrilled in 2014 to have won First Place at the Feathered Quill Book Awards for I'm So Hollywood in the Summer Saltz series. A native of Sydney, Australia, I currently reside in Los Angeles with my husband and two crazy-sweet boys. I look forward to working with you on your next project!

Elyse
