Thank you so much for stopping by!

I invite you to browse through this site to see examples of custom, hand-painted watercolor portraits of homes, along with digital backgrounds for newborn photography and children's book illustrations — I would love to discuss your personalized watercolor home portrait or other illustration project you might have in mind.

With a BFA in Illustration, my professional experience includes character design, prop design, and storyboards, working for more than 10 years in the animation industry in Los Angeles, where I collaborated with creative teams at the Warner Bros. and Nickelodeon studios. My credits include the feature film Space Jam and Nickelodeon TV shows such as "Dora the Explorer," "Hey Arnold," and "Oswald the Octopus."

As a children's book illustrator, I was thrilled in 2014 to win first place at the Feathered Quill Book Awards for I'm So Hollywood in the Summer Saltz book series. Several of the books I've illustrated — including the recently published The Magic Red Dress and Theodore and Hazel and the Bird — featured in the February 2019 Alumni Art Exhibit at La Sierra University's Brandstater Gallery, in Riverside, CA.

A native of Sydney, Australia, I currently reside in Los Angeles with my husband and two crazy-sweet boys.

I look forward to working with you on your next project! Contact me.


-Elyse
